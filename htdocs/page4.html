About this Page

This section, which is less centered around typhoon preparation, serves as entertainment for those
stuck at home due to a typhoon and bad weather, and as pastime for bored readers.


The Necklace by Guy de Maupassant

This short story narrates the mishaps of an image-obsessed woman and an expensive necklace that she borrows.

Ozymandias by Percy Bysshe Shelley

This classic sonnet retells a traveler's description of a statue buried in a desert.

The Gift of The Magi by O. Henry

This sentimental story tells of a couple buying Christmas gifts for each other.

A Dream Within A Dream by Edgar Allan Poe

This hypnotizing poem, which is more cryptic than the other entries, blurs between reality and dreams.
