Typhoon Odette, also known as Typhoon Rai

  • Status: 2 months after

  • Date in the Philippines: December 16 - December 18

  • Category: 5, supertyphoon

  • Death Toll: 405 dead as of December 31, 2021; 82 missing

  • Alarm Level: The storm has already dissipated, but affected areas still have not recovered.
