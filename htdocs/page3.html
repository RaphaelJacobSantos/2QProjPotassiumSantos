Super Typhoon Rai

  • Also Known As: Super Typoon Odette in the Philippines

  • Status: 2 months after dissipation; affected areas still recovering

  • Date in the Philippines: December 16 - December 18

  • Strength: Category 5 supertyphoon; maximum winds at 195 kph.

  • Death Toll: 405 dead as of December 31, 2021.

  • Alarm Level: Affected areas still have not recovered.
